Game Projects

The Flying Dutchman

The Flying Dutchman is a 3D pirate-themed, first-person mystery game focused around traveling to different islands in the sky and discovering treasure with your flying pirate ship. The game serves as my Capstone Project to graduate from UCSC in 2025.

Lead Level Designer & Co-Producer at Lost At Sea

ONEs

ONES is a fast paced first-person online multiplayermovement shooter. Use unique weapons and and learn to move quickly to avoid being hit while in intense combat against your opponent.

Level Designer (Dockyard) for DropZone Interactive

AppleHead

AppleHead is a 2D run and game demo inspired by the 2017 hit game Cuphead. Players fight against a boss with unique stages and attacks using only their wits and their skills. The project served as my group's final for CMPM 146, showcasing Behavior Tree Game AI.

Art, Programmer and Sound Design

Lil Guy

Lil Guy is a 2D platforming side-scrolling demo developed in Phaser Java. Players traverse through two levels, avoid obstacles, collecting keys, and stomping on enemies. The project served as my group's final for CMPM 120, showcasing our game development experinece.

Programmer, Effects and Sound Designer

Gem Wrangler

Gem Wrangler is a 2D rhythm game made in GDevelop. Players memorize their crane operating system and sort out gems into boxes to satisfy work quotas. The game served as my group's final for CMPM 80K, pushing us to the test of producing a game in a short time.

Art and Storyboard


Counter-Strike Maps

Taisha

Taisha is a bomb-defusal map set in the Kansai region of Japan. Players navigate through a vertical-based layout reminiscent of real-life locations such as the Fushimi Inari Taisha and the Ginzan Onsen.
Taisha was entered into the Mapcore Big Adventures contest, making it into Stage 2 of the contest, making the top 57/250+ maps.
Credits:
ethurs, Axesh_ - Environment Art
Eagle One Studios - Additional Modeling, Sound Design

Villaggio

Villaggio is a bomb-defusal map set in the beautiful coastal town of Manarola, Italy. With a huge emphasis on player verticality, the map seeks to challenge players while providing balanced, competitive gameplay.
The map has been a passion project I've worked on and off since 2018, with this version encapsulating all the best parts of past versions.

Traffick

Traffick is a bomb-defusal map set in Central Mexico. Players traverse the compact architecture of this small town housing illegal narcotics.
